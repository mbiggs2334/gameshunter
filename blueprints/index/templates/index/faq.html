GamesHunter FAQ

  • What is GamesHunter?

    • GamesHunter is a project for my SpringBoard education course. This is my first ever website where I built everything you see from scratch. (With the exception of the game information of course, that information comes from API's).

  • How do I make a forum post?

    • To make a forum post you need to head to a game's detail page and you'll see a button labeled "Let's talk about it". Alternatively, you'll see a similar button when mousing over the cards on the Game search page.

  • Why don't you have anymore FAQ questions?

    • As previously stated, this is a project for my SpringBoard education course. The main focus is the ability to build something, not neccesarily the content itself. Plus I can't think of anymore. Three seems like a good number to stop at.
